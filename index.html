Yo! My name is Daniel Figueroa, a first year AGPM student from Los Angeles, California. My interest in art and computers started pretty much since my consciousness awoke. Though I have very little time or inspiration these days, I still enjoy doodling and do some occasional concept art to keep my technique from regressing. When I'm not making traditional art, I do graphic design for mostly logos, covers, and clothing. Lately I've been experimenting with different art forms like watercolor and sewing, so I hope to add this class to my repertoire!
